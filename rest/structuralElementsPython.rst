###############################################
Structural elements -- the *Python convention*
###############################################

..

--------------------

    `#` with overline, for parts

    `*` with overline, for chapters

    `=`, for sections

    `-`, for subsections

    `^`, for subsubsections

    `"`, for paragraphs
